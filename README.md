# TireTally
TireTally helps you keep a tally of tire costs and miles driven.

Available for iOS 26 and iPadOS 26.

Cars have Sets of Tires for easy editing. You record Tire Events like swaps and rotations by the set or by the tire. Summary data is presented. 

## Privacy
Your data syncs between your devices via iCloud and is not accessible to the developer or any third party.

**TireTally does not connect to your vehicle, does not read tire pressures or treadwear data, and does not transmit your information to any external servers.**
